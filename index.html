About the club

Humanitarian leadership rooted in empathy.

We mobilize campus resources to co-create welfare initiatives: emergency response, seasonal relief, digital literacy, health camps, advocacy marches, and psychosocial care.

Mission

Champion dignity for marginalized communities through sustained student-led action.

Values

Integrity, transparency, inclusive collaboration, and a culture of care.

Impact

Emergency relief cells, winter warmth drives, health camps, and advocacy coalitions.
